List of words to watch is the list of text patterns which, if they appear in a message, should ensure that the message is not automatically approved and is stored in WebSTUMP for human review.

These words may be an indication of a potential spam, or a flame etc. Perhaps, for many groups, putting common profanities, as well as things like XXX and 1-900, is a good strategy. Note however, that there is generally no reason to forbid the use of profanity, as such.

Example:

happy99.exe
fuck
shit
cunt
XXX
1-900
1-800
make money fast
My advice is to watch your group for patterns appearing in offtopic posts, and use them to your advantage to help you with filtering.
