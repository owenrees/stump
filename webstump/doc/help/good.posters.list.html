Good posters list is the list of email addresses (no names) of trusted posters. All messages coming from "trusted posters", provided that they do not fall within the purview of (other filters), are automatically approved.

Exzample:

goodguy@aol.com
nicegal@my-dejanews.com
racingfan@webtv.net
Using a preapproved list extensively does two good things:
  • Reduces your workload by up to 90%
  • Dramatically improves timeliness of discussions and creates a more discussion-like atmosphere.
